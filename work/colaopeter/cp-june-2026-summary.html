🏡 Colao & Peter — June 2026 Performance Summary

Prepared July 10, 2026  ·  GA4  ·  Google Search Console

Overview

June was the strongest traffic month of 2026 so far. Total sessions reached 2,041 — up 44% from May — driven by a combination of organic search holding steady, organic social continuing to grow, and new paid Meta activity adding incremental reach. Quality held up: organic search visitors engaged at 65%, and AI-referred visitors (ChatGPT, Gemini) engaged at over 70%.

The standout story of the month isn't the traffic number — it's the pool renovations article. Published in late June, Luxury Pool Renovations: Reimagining What You Already Have generated 8 conversions from just 30 sessions. That's a 26.7% conversion rate, the highest of any page on the site. The content is working exactly as intended.

⚠️ Overall engagement rate dipped to 30.8% (from 42.2% in May). The cause is mix shift — Organic Social (9% ER, 548 sessions) and Paid Social (6% ER, 281 sessions) together represent 41% of all sessions and dilute the sitewide average. Organic search quality is unchanged.

Paid Social (Instagram + Facebook ads) is new this month at 281 sessions. Engagement is low at 6%, which is typical for top-of-funnel paid social — the goal is reach and brand exposure, not immediate conversion. Worth tracking over time to see if any paid social visitors return via direct.

Referral and AI traffic combined brought 65 sessions at 63% engagement — small in volume but the most engaged visitors on the site outside organic search. ChatGPT alone sent 10 sessions averaging nearly 8 minutes on site.

Recommended Next Steps
1

Build on the pool renovations article's conversion rate

A 26.7% conversion rate from 30 sessions is a strong signal. The article is reaching visitors who are already in consideration mode. The priority now is getting more of those visitors to it — internal links from the pool service pages, a featured placement on the homepage, and a GSC review to see which queries are already landing there.

2

Evaluate Paid Social efficiency as data accumulates

281 sessions from Meta ads this month at 6% engagement. Too early to draw conclusions — one month isn't enough. Track whether any paid social visitors return via direct or convert on a second visit. Review cost-per-session and whether the audience targeting is reaching the right Northern Virginia market.

3

Second long-form article — pool houses or outdoor living

The pool renovations article proved the format works. The next content investment should follow the same pattern: a specific topic that current Colao & Peter clients are researching, written in depth. Pool houses, pergolas, or outdoor kitchens are all topics with search volume and clear connection to C&P's actual work.
