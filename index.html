Business Model

Built for direct client services, not public software access

We work with small and mid-size businesses across industries including industrial products, home improvement, and e-commerce. Google Ads API access supports internal agency operations only and is never resold, published as a product, or exposed to clients.

Agency, not SaaS

Shiba & Associates provides managed marketing services under direct client agreements. We do not operate a public ads platform or developer product.

MCC-Based Management

Client Google Ads accounts are managed through a Google Ads Manager Account so reporting, optimization, and campaign maintenance can be handled centrally by authorized personnel.

Internal API Use Only

The Google Ads API is used only for internal reporting, campaign changes, anomaly monitoring, and workflow automation. Clients receive services and reports, not API access.
