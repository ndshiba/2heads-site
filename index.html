Core Service

Google Ads management with reporting built in

Shiba & Associates manages Google Ads campaigns on behalf of business clients under a Google Ads Manager Account. We use the Google Ads API to support internal reporting, campaign management, optimization, and account monitoring across authorized accounts.

Search Campaigns

Keyword research, match type strategy, ad copywriting, bid management, negative keyword review, and ongoing optimization based on search term performance.

Shopping & Performance Max

Product feed strategy, campaign structure, bidding, and audience segmentation for e-commerce accounts running Google Shopping and Performance Max.

Reporting & Attribution

Operating Model

Built for managed service work, not software resale

Shiba & Associates operates as a hands-on marketing partner for a small portfolio of businesses. The work is account-specific, strategy-led, and handled directly by authorized personnel. This site exists to describe the agency, its services, and how client campaign data is handled.

Agency, not SaaS

There is no public dashboard, no self-serve campaign workflow, and no multi-tenant software product. Engagements begin through direct outreach and service agreements.

Authorized Account Access

Accounts are accessed only after a client links our manager account or grants the permissions required for the work we are performing.

Focused Client Portfolio

Work typically supports small and mid-size businesses in categories such as industrial products, home improvement, trailers, and e-commerce.
